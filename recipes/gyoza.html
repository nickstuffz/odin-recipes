Gyoza fom TJs

Description

TJs Pork Gyoza Potstickers are a delicious and cheap staple of the Nick Ng diet. Here is how he prepares them.

Ingredients

  • potstickers
  • olive oil
  • apple cider vinegar
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Drizzle olive oil onto large pan and heat until hot.
  2. Place potstickers onto pan and cook on medium for 3min.
  3. Add water into pan until 1/3 of potsticker height submerged, cover with lid and cook for 3min.
  4. Remove lid and unstick potstickers. Heat on high for 1min.
  5. Creat dipping sauce of 1/3 soy sauce 2/3 apple cider vinegar.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
